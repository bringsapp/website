How does it work?

Individuals who are either travelling (referred to as travellers) from one location to other or those in the need (referred to as requesters) of having an item brought between locations, can easily create an entry on our website. Upong logging in, users will be able to discover these entries, and can search for the specific entries based on the locations.

Once you find the entry that is of interest, just click on the poster's name to get to their profile and send a message. Only verified users will be able to send messages to others.

FAQs:

Q. Is brings free to use, right now?
A. Yes.
Q. As a requester, am I supposed to pay to the other person?
A. Yes.
Q. As a requester, how much am I supposed to pay to the other person?
A. Totally depends on how much the other person agrees on.
Q. Are users verified?
A. It's just their phone number that is verified right now. We are planning to add ID verification soon. User's profile can be visited to see if their phone is verified or not.
