How does it work?


Individuals who are either travelling (referred to as traveller) from one location to another or those in need (referred to as requesters) of having an item brought between locations, can easily create an entry on our service. Upon logging in, users will be able to discover these entries and can search for the specific entries based on the locations.

Once you find the entry that is of interest, just click on the poster's name to get to their profile and send a message. Only verified users will be able to send messages to others.

FAQs:

Q. As a requester, am I supposed to pay to the other person (traveller)?
A. Yes. But, if a traveller agrees to bring your parcel for free, brings would not object that.
Q. As a requester, how much am I supposed to pay to the other person?
A. Depends on how much the other person agrees on.
Q. Are users verified?
A. It's just their contact number that is verified right now. We are planning to add ID verification soon. User's profile can be visited to see if their contact number is verified or not.
Q. Why is push notification not working on iOS?
A. To make sure that web push notifications are working properly, please add the web application to your home screen using chrome web browser. And you should be able to susbcribe to push notifications by opening brings from home screen and then going to messages menu.
